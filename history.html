History

1961

IBM creates the first digital speech recognizer, the IBM Shoebox which recognized 16 words and 9 digits.

1976

The Harpy Program was created by Carnegie Mellon. Harpy could understand 1000 words.

1997

The company Dragon had launched Dragon Dictate, the first speech recognition

1997 - 2000

Microsoft releases Clippy, their first VA. It was later removed in 2000.

2003

Microsoft introduced a speech recognition as a new feature for Office XP.

2011

Apple introduced Siri, the first modern digital virtual assistant to be installed on a smartphone, a new feature that came out on the iPhone S4.

2012

Google launched a feature called Google Now, an app for Android and IOS.

2014

Mircosoft introduced Cortana, their first VA.

2014 - 2016

Amazon has introduced a virtual assistant, Alexa. In March 2016, they released a byproduct called Amazon Echo Dot.

2016

Google introduced Google Assistant and launched Google Home.

2017

Amazon released the 2nd generation of the Amazon Echo Dot.

2017

Samsung introduced Bixby, a new feature on the Galaxy S8 release.
