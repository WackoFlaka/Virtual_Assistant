Virtual Assistant

What is Artificial Intelligence?

An Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is any computer program that replicates a form of intelligence. Most use complex algorithms and a large set of data to make decisions. A form of AI more crucial to Virtual Assistants are known as “narrow AI”. These Ais are very efficient at precise tasks, but they lack the ability to generalize and be abstract like humans.

What is Virtual Assistance?

A Virtual Assistant, or VA, is a form of AI that uses data gathered over time along with several complex algorithms to form intelligent responses to human questions. They use language processing, process automation, machine learning, and the internet to gather more information. They also gather information and develop patterns from their users to form better answers. They stem off of the narrow AI mentioned above, and are the basis of what this site talks about.
